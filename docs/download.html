Who are we?

The world in 2019 is not the same than 1999. A lot of things have changed and everything becomes as a dream.
Some could say it is witchcraft, but let us tell you that it is not! Our time is just amazing and we wonder how within 100 years the earth will be.
Today we do music in Digital Platform, isn't amazing? We will never know how far this Digital way will lead us.
Lucky are our children.

Boyo Lifestyle is a Record Label created in 2017 in Gabon as BOYO Production by John Chirst Bouma.
In March 2019 Smirfylu signed under Boyo Lifestyle and release is first song of his future album Colors in May 2019.
We actually located in South Africa where we start our new journey in the Digital Game.
